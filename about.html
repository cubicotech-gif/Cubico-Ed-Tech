About Cubico

A creative EdTech agency based in Karachi, Pakistan. We build technology that makes education better.

We build EdTech that actually works.

Cubico Technologies is a Karachi-based EdTech agency specializing in digital solutions for educational institutions. From Moodle LMS setups to 2D/3D animations, school management systems to e-learning content — we deliver end-to-end solutions that help institutions teach better.

We serve conventional schools, Islamic institutions, madrassas, universities, coaching centers, and corporate training programs. Everything we build supports English, Urdu, and Arabic — including full RTL layout support.

Our team combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of how education works in Pakistan and the wider Muslim world. We don't just deliver software — we deliver solutions that fit your institution's culture, language, and goals.
